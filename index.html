About Me

Scott is an aspiring leader, a doctoral candidate in the field of Leadership Studies from the University of San Diego, a charismatic and devoted member of the Deaf community at large. Scott has been in numerous leadership roles ever since his undergraduate years at Gallaudet University, the only university for the Deaf in the world. Positions include, but are not limited to;

Formerly

  • President of the Campus Student Media
  • Special Student Assistant to the Dean's office
  • President of the Alpha Sigma Pi Fraternity

Currently

  • Program Manager of Daytime Recovery Center at San Diego
  • Disability Rights California Board of Directors
  • The Alpha Sigma Pi INC Board of Directors

Currently, Scott devotes the majority of his day at the local non-profit where he is the Director of the Advocacy department. After completing his degree requirements, Scott aims to expand his leadership experiences, by honing in his skills as a Leadership Coach, and ultimately, become the leader of his community. As a leader, Scott understands the nooks and crannies that come with the position, and is committed to creating a culture of healthy and collaborative communities.
